The threats are real

Children face dangers that evolve faster than parental controls can keep up. Guardeon uses AI to close the gap.

🎭

Online Grooming

Predators spend weeks building trust before exploitation. Our NLP models detect grooming language patterns in real time.

Avg grooming time: 8 minutes on some platforms

Cyberbullying

Persistent harassment that follows children from school to home. Guardeon identifies escalating patterns before crisis.

37% of children experience cyberbullying

Harmful Content

Unsolicited explicit images, self-harm content, and violence. On-device image classification flags threats without cloud upload.

How Guardeon works

AI-powered protection that respects privacy. No screenshots. No surveillance. Just smart, on-device detection.

01
🔒

On-Device Analysis

All detection happens locally on the device. Messages and images never leave the phone. Privacy by design.

02
🧠

AI Risk Engine

Multi-language NLP models trained on real grooming, bullying, and threat patterns. Continuously learning, always improving.

03
🚨

Smart Alerts

Parents and guardians receive context-aware alerts with risk levels — not raw messages. Children's privacy stays intact.

Our principles

Technology this sensitive demands radical transparency.

🔓 Open Source

Every line of code is public. Every model is auditable. Trust is earned through transparency, not marketing.

🛡️ Privacy First

On-device processing. No cloud uploads. No data harvesting. We protect children without surveilling them.

🌍 Non-Profit

Guardeon is a registered Danish forening (association). No shareholders. No ads. Surplus funds the mission.

🔬 Evidence-Based

Built on peer-reviewed research. We publish our findings. If our approach is wrong, we want to know first.

🇪🇺 EU-Compliant

GDPR-native. Aligned with the EU AI Act and Digital Services Act. Designed for European standards from day one.

🤝 Collaborative

Working with schools, municipalities, NGOs, and international bodies. This problem is too big for one organisation.

Security & Trust, Europe
Tanya spent over a decade in trust, safety, and security at one of the world's largest technology platforms — working at the intersection of infrastructure security, content integrity, and platform risk across billions of users.

An active contributor to IEEE standards for child and youth safety in the digital age, she saw firsthand how the gap between policy and technology leaves children exposed. Guardeon was born from a simple conviction: the tools to protect children online should be open, free, and owned by no one.

Based in Denmark. Mother of two. Building what she wishes already existed.

Guardeon — from "guard" (to protect) and "eon" (an age, an era). Technology that protects children not just today, but for generations.

Guardeon means protection that endures. We build open-source AI that keeps children safe online — not as a product, but as a public good. For this generation and every one after.
